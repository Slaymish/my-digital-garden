## Determine the Value of the Unknown in Each Equation

1. $2(3-h)-6=-5h$
	$6-2h-6=-5h$
	$-2h+0=-5h$
	$-3h=0$
	$h=0$

2. $7+9d=7d+3$
	$4+2d=0$
	$2d=-4$
	$d=-4/2$
	$d=-2$

3. $-2(4+3y)=-2(4+y)$
	$-8-6y=-8-2y$
	$y=0$
