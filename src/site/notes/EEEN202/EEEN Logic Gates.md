## Exclusive OR and Exclusive NOR Circuits

- XOR produces a HIGH output when two inputs are opposite
- XNOR produces a HIGH output whenever the two inputs are the same level
- XOR and XNOR are opposite

$XOR = \neg XNOR$
$XNOR = \neg XOR$
